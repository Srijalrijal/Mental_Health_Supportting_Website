Manko Sahara

Our Mission

"Manko Sahara" is dedicated to breaking the stigma surrounding mental health in Nepal by providing culturally relevant resources, education, and support. Our mission is to empower individuals with the knowledge, tools, and connections needed to foster mental well-being, create a compassionate community, and ensure that everyone has access to mental health care, regardless of their location or background. We strive to collaborate with local professionals, utilize culturally sensitive approaches, and promote open dialogue to build a society where mental health is understood, respected, and prioritized.
