Manko Sahara

About Us

Welcome to Maanko Sahara, a platform dedicated to raising mental health awareness and providing accessible support within Nepal. In a society where discussions around mental well-being are often overshadowed by stigma and misconceptions, Maanko Sahara strives to be a safe space that fosters open conversations, education, and empowerment.

Vision

Our vision is to build a society where mental health is understood, respected, and prioritized just as much as physical health. We believe that everyone, regardless of their background or location, deserves access to mental health resources and support.
