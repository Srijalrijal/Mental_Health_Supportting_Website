HelpLine Directory

  • 1. Nepal Suicide Prevention Hotline (TUTH Helpline)

    Operating Hours: 24/7 Contact: 1166 (Toll-Free)
    • Service: Suicide prevention support, counseling, and referrals for those in emotional distress.

    2. TPO Nepal (Transcultural Psychosocial Organization)

    Operating Hours: Weekdays (Office hours) Contact: +977-1-5358671 / 5358672
    • Service: Mental health counseling, psychosocial support, and emotional assistance.

  • 3. Koshish Helpline

    Operating Hours: 8 AM – 8 PM Contact: +977-16600133200 (Toll-Free)
    • Service: Mental health and suicide prevention support, including outreach for those in need of urgent psychological help.

    4. Psychiatric Hospital, Lagankhel, Lalitpur

    Operating Hours: 24/7 emergency psychiatric services. Contact: +977-56-532628
    • Service: Emergency mental health services, including crisis intervention and support.

    5.Patan Academy of Health Sciences (PAHS) - Mental Health Unit

    Nobel Medical College – Psychiatry Department
    Operating Hours: Weekdays (Office hours) Contact: +977-1-5522295
    • Service: Mental health and counseling services, offering support to those in psychological distress.

    6. Asha Suicide Prevention Helpline

    Operating Hours: 10 AM – 7 PMi Contact: +977-9821285339 / +977-9821036801
    • Services: Mental health services, psychological counseling, psychiatric treatment.

    7. Crisis Helpline at Maiti Nepal

    Operating Hours: 24/7 Contact: +977-1-4492904 / 4494816
    • Service: Support for women and children, including mental health crises, abuse, and trauma-related cases.

    8. Voice of Children - Mental Health and Crisis Support

    Operating Hours: Office hours Contact: +977-1-5551553
    • Provides support for children in emotional distress, abuse recovery, and crisis counseling.

    9. Nepal Red Cross Society - Psycho-social Support Helpline

    Operating Hours: Office hours Contact: +977-11431043
    • Service: Psychological first aid and crisis counseling for individuals experiencing mental health challenges.
